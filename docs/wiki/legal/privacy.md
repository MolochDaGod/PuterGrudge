## 1. Introduction

CloudPilot AI Studio ("we", "our", "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Puter user ID (provided by Puter authentication)
- Email address (if provisioned)
- Username and display preferences

### 2.2 Usage Data
- API requests and response times
- Feature usage patterns
- Error logs (anonymized)

### 2.3 Content Data
- Files you upload or create
- AI conversations and prompts
- Application code and configurations

### 2.4 Technical Data
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- IP address (for security purposes)

## 3. How We Use Information

### 3.1 Service Operation
- Authenticate users via Puter
- Process AI model requests
- Store and serve your content
- Provide customer support

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns (aggregated)
- Identify and fix bugs
- Develop new features

### 3.3 Security
- Detect and prevent abuse
- Enforce rate limits
- Investigate security incidents

---

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Primary Storage
All user data is stored using **Puter.js** cloud infrastructure:
- File system: `puter.fs`
- Key-value store: `puter.kv`
- Application hosting: `puter.hosting`

### 4.2 Storage Location
Data is stored on Puter's cloud servers. See [Puter's Privacy Policy](https://puter.com/privacy) for infrastructure details.

### 4.3 Retention
- Active account data: Retained while account is active
- Deleted data: Removed within 30 days
- Anonymized logs: May be retained for analytics

---

## 5. Data Sharing

### 5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

### 5.2 Third-Party Services
We share necessary data with:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Puter.js** | Cloud infrastructure, AI | User ID, content, files |
| **AI Model Providers** | Process AI requests | Prompts, context |

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

## 6. AI Model Privacy

### 6.1 Prompts and Responses
- Your AI prompts are sent to model providers
- Responses are returned directly to you
- We may cache responses to improve performance

### 6.2 Model Provider Policies
Different AI models have different privacy practices. Major providers include:
- OpenAI (GPT models)
- Anthropic (Claude models)
- Google (Gemini models)
- Meta (Llama models)
- Mistral AI

Consult each provider's privacy policy for details.

### 6.3 No Training on Your Data
We do not use your prompts or content to train AI models.
